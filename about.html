A little bit about me...

My journey

My journey into software development began back in college when I took a class called "The Fundamentals of Programming." I didn’t know it at the time, but that class planted the seed that would eventually grow into my passion for software development.

Since then, I’ve been on a self-learning path filled with late-night coding sessions, grinding through LeetCode, and fumbling my way through interviews. Eventually, after some trial, error, and a few “Thank you for your interest...” emails, I was lucky enough to be selected as an apprentice at one of the world’s top telecommunications companies—Verizon.

Which brings us to where I am now—partnering with Multiverse as a Verizon apprentice, working to sharpen my software engineering skills.

Hobbies & Interests

When I'm not working, I enjoy reading and watching TV. I’m a fan of fantasy, sci-fi, and horror, whether it’s in books, movies, or TV shows. Right now, I’m rereading Brandon Sanderson’s *Stormlight Archive* in preparation for his new release later this year.

I also like staying active, spending time at the gym, and enjoying outdoor activities like hiking or going to the beach.
